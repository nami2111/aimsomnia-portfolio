About

My artistic journey began with a fascination for AI-generated art, which sparked a deeper desire to create on my own. I started learning digital art through tools like Photoshop and GoPaint, then expanded into motion graphics using After Effects. Drawn to the charm of simpler, more expressive visuals, I eventually explored pixel art with Aseprite. Each medium I experimented with gave me new perspectives on visual storytelling and helped shape my creative approach. Outside the world of art, I also spend time learning general-purpose programming—exploring logic and structure in code as part of my interest in technology. This collection is a reflection of my ongoing journey through art: full of exploration, experimentation, and continuous growth.
